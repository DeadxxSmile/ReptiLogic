Core workflow

Everything you need to manage a modern reptile collection.

Designed around the same dark, focused design language as the app itself, with fast access to the pieces breeders actually use day to day.

Collection

  • Track animals with photos, morphs, weight, DOB, acquisition info, and notes
  • Auto-generated Animal IDs or enter your own
  • Grid and list views with filtering by species, sex, status, and search
  • Lineage builds automatically with clickable family tree support
  • Bulk import from CSV

Health

  • Per-animal dashboard with weight trend chart
  • Log issues, medications, vet visits, treatment, and follow-up
  • Overdue reminders for feeding and weighing
  • History stays attached to the animal record over time

Breeding

  • Full pairing lifecycle from Planned to Hatched or Born
  • Clutch logging with incubation details and offspring records
  • Add Babies Wizard for rapid hatchling entry
  • Suggested pairings based on genetics in your active collection

Genetics Calculator

  • Supports recessive, co-dominant, dominant, line-bred, and sex-linked traits
  • Allele complex support including BEL, Yellow Belly, 8-Ball, and more
  • Banana and Coral Glow male maker and female maker handling
  • Outcome cards show morph names, odds, expected counts, and sex ratio notes

Morph Library

  • Built-in libraries for Ball Python, Western Hognose, Leopard Gecko, and Bearded Dragon
  • Health concern flags where appropriate
  • Add custom morphs and use them in the calculator automatically

Backup, Export, Reports

  • Automatic ZIP backups on open or close with configurable retention
  • One-click restore from saved backups
  • CSV export for collection, breeding records, and morphs
  • Professional husbandry reports with photo, pedigree, weights, and feeding history
